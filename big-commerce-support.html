Sync your BigCommerce Store with Handshake

Seamlessly sync your products, customers, and orders between BigCommerce and Handshake. Syncs can be run manually, or on a set schedule. To get started, you’ll need to have a Handshake Pro account and an API key. You can find your API key here.

Products

Export to Handshake

Products that exist in BigCommerce will be synced to Handshake. Update the products in BigCommerce, and the changes will be reflected in Handshake. Your product tree will be updated.

In addition to products, these related objects will also export to Handshake:

  • Inventory
  • Categories
  • Options and variants

Customers

Sync between BigCommerce/Handshake

Your customer list will sync to Handshake. In addition, you can also create customers in Handshake, and have them sync back. This is useful, for instance, if you are at a tradeshow and sell to a new customer, or have a sales rep in the field

In addition, these customer-related objects objects will also sync to Handshake, or sync from Handshake for new customers:

  • Billing/shipping addresses
  • Customer groups

Orders

Import to BigCommerce

Orders written in Handshake will import to BigCommerce. You can configure at which point in your workflow orders will import to BigCommerce. The available statuses are:

  • Open
  • Hold for Confirm
  • Confirmed
  • Processing
  • Complete
